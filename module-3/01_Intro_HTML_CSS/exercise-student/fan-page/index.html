Radiohead

Interesting Facts

  • Radiohead formed in Abingdon, Oxfordshire in 1985.
  • Their original name was On a Friday.
  • The band has sold more than 30 million albums.
  • All the band members are multi-instumentalists and will often switch instruments when performing.
